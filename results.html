Scores range from 0 to 100 for each dimension. Higher scores indicate stronger competency, except for Stress where higher scores indicate higher perceived stress levels.

The 5QS+ Composite Score averages IQ, EQ, AQ, SQ, and DQ (Stress is reported separately). This composite reflects overall entrepreneurial readiness as conceptualised in the 5QS+ framework (Nanade & Dash, 2026).

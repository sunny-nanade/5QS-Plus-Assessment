5QS+ Entrepreneurial Competency Assessment

Reframing Competency Assessment in the Digital Age

Research Tool • Open-Source Assessment Platform

About this Assessment

This platform operationalises the 5QS+ Framework, a research-validated model for assessing entrepreneurial competency across five core dimensions plus stress. It extends the established 4QS model (Nanade et al., 2021; Nanade et al., 2026) by integrating Digital Quotient (DQ) — a critical new dimension capturing AI literacy, data analytics competency, digital ethics, and cyber awareness.

The framework is grounded in Human Capital Theory (Becker, 1993), Experiential Learning Theory (Kolb, 1984), and Theory of Planned Behaviour (Ajzen, 1991), providing a comprehensive, technology-enabled approach to competency assessment for the digital economy.

What to Expect

The assessment consists of six modules administered sequentially:

  1. IQ Test — 15 questions, 8 minutes (Adapted from Wonderlic Cognitive Ability Test)
  2. EQ Test — 15 questions, 5 minutes (Based on EQ-i 2.0 Model; Bar-On)
  3. AQ Test — 15 questions, 5 minutes (Based on CORE Model; Stoltz, 1997)
  4. SQ Test — 15 questions, 5 minutes (Based on SISRI-24; King, 2008)
  5. DQ Test — 30 questions, 8 minutes (Developed for 5QS+; Chen & Ifenthaler, 2023; van Laar et al., 2017; OECD, 2023)
  6. Stress Assessment — 10 questions, 3 minutes (Based on PSS-10; Cohen et al.)

Total estimated time: ~34 minutes. You may skip any module and proceed to the next. Your results will include a 6-axis radar chart, composite breakdowns, and a final 5QS+ score.

Privacy & Data Use

All data collected through this platform is used solely for academic research and educational purposes. Responses are transmitted securely and stored in compliance with institutional research ethics guidelines. No personally identifiable information will be disclosed in any publication. Participation is voluntary and you may withdraw at any time.
